Gary D'Souza
Co-Founder

knew he wanted to be a Football Coach at age 12, and every step he’s taken since, has been in a direction to further accomplish his vision of football for all. Experiencing his first taste of Coaching in the United States of America at age 19, to going on to study his Bachelors in Sports Health and Fitness Management from Malaysia and went on to graduate with honours,

Gary D'Souza
Co-Founder

He scored a much coveted internship at the prestigious Arsenal Soccer Schools, Dubai. From running their operations in the day and coaching in the evenings, Gary spent 5 years at the Arsenal Soccer School enjoying every single minute, honoured to be working along side Coaches and players from over 45 different nationalities.

Gary D'Souza
Co-Founder

Highly influenced and inspired by the Senior Coaches at Arsenal Soccer School Gary left Dubai in 2015 to bring the best of football coaching back to his home country, India. He went on to co-found Kopana Football School with his school mate Baptist Fernandes soon after. As Head Coach as well as manager of everything that happens on the football pitch, Gary runs a progressive and passionate school.
