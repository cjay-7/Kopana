Our Story

Founded on 18th September 2015,
Kopana Football School is an after-school football-training program for children,
from the ages of 2.5 till 18.

2009

Juhu Beach, Mumbai

Coach Gary, working at the Arsenal Soccer Schools, Dubai, comes to India (from 2009 - 2015) for a month each year, to coach 75 underprivileged youth at a local beach. The team is called ‘Project Play’. It is here that Gary realises the need for a football training school.

September 18

2015

Gary moves back to India First Session as Kopana Football School

Armed with his vision and the top 30 students at Project Play, Gary and Baptist start the Kopana Youth Squad, who begin training as Kopana Football School on September 18, at a local school ground. Soon the level of coaching and play catches the eye of parents.

October

2015

First group of students enroll

Kopana Football School holds its first training session with 2 students, forming the U - 10 batch. Soon to follow is an U - 14 batch in November comprising of 10 students.

2016

We pick up pace

Kopana Football School starts U - 6 and U - 8 batches in January, followed by the first U - 12 batch in March. Based on a parents recommendation, 30 children join this batch at a go! Kopana Football School plays its first multi-team tournament in September 2016. It is a wonderfully bonding experience.

2017

Official Partnership with FCV, UK

The year starts on a great note; two Kopana Football School students get the opportunity to try out at Bengaluru FC. In May 2017, FCV UK and Kopana Football School sign a partnership. Our student, Hardik Bhat attends the FCV Camp and is voted Outstanding Player.

2017

Our first live game as #kopanafamily

128 members of the Kopana Family - students, parents and Kopana staff watch their first profootball Indian Super League game live in the stadium! What excitement! Kopana Football School has 300 enrolled students! We launch a Mothers batch based on demand by Kopana parents.

2018

350 Students and counting

In April, we set a record for the most players training at one time - 117 children on the pitch! In July, our Coaching Staff hold an outstation training camp for Billimoria High School, Panchgani. In September, Kopana Football School reaches 350 students on their roster!

October

2018

Youth Team progresses in the MDFA League

The Kopana Football School Youth Team are practising long and hard. But the discipline is paying off as we qualify for the play-offs in the 2nd Division of the Mumbai District Football Association (MDFA) league. The chase is on!

January

2019

Youth Squad win their first championship!

Ex-Scottish League Player & Coach Graham Birell visits Kopana Football School as visiting faculty, and supports our Youth Team in winning their first Championship at the Billimoria Football League! Our first time being crowned Champions!

2019

Youth Team awarded Runners-Up at MDFA

The Youth Team plays a thrilling final game to be awarded Runners-Up at the MDFA League. Kopana Football School goes on to sign its first long term ground contract at Nanavati Turf, our current home ground. In June, Kopana Football School organises its first outstation football camp for 45 students.

2019

Project Play at Prem Sadan

In October, Kopana Football School launches weekly coaching sessions for 80 underprivileged girls at the Prem Sadan Children’s Home and Orphanage. It is our dream to have them flourish like our Project Play Youth Team. Kopana Football School ends the year with 400 students as part of the growing #kopanafamily.

2020

Kopana Youth Team retains its title!

The Kopana Youth Team retains its championship title at the Billimoria Football League, at the start of the year! Coach Graham Birell visits us for a second fantastic season of coaching.

2009 2015 2016 2017 2018 2019 2020

What started as a class for 2 students, has now blossomed into a buzzing sports club with more than 400 children who learn the nuances of football in a fun, professional, safe and growth-centric environment.

At Kopana Football School, we pride ourselves on offering progressive football coaching for students from all backgrounds and all abilities.

Our Moto

01

Mission

To spread the joy of football whilst inculcating life skills and socio - emotional learning in as many young individuals as possible, so that everyone who wants to play football can.

02

Vision

We aim to develop players to excel at the traditional 11-aside format of the game, in order to set up competitive teams in the future. Our objective is to be a pro-club within the next 10 years.

03

Objective

To position Kopana Football School as the premier platform for football-related youth development in India, by offering programs with an integrated, hands on approach to sport, across a variety of age groups and competition levels.
